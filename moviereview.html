Movie Review

    Agent (2023) Telugu Movie: What's Behind King Nagarjuna's son Akhil scored a decent hit with Most Eligible Bachelor and is now on a mission to get a mass image with his spy thriller Agent. Akhil is aiming for the starts with Agent as the film directed by Surender Reddy is releasing in various languages. Akhil transformed his physique working hard for months while Surender Reddy braved the covid virus and producers overcame the spiralling budget to release the film in theatres. The action-packed teaser and trailer increased expectations on the film. Let us find out whether Akhil created a mass mania with Agent. The film's OTT rights have been bagged by Sony Liv and streaming will be after the theatrical run.

    Agent Movie: Story Review Agent Story is all about a youngster who aspires to become a RAW agent and how he resolves that fight between God and the Devil. Rikki aka Rama Krishna (Akhil), is an ethical hacker dreams of becoming a RAW agent since his childhood. But he fails in his attempts to clear the exam due to his weird ways. But a sudden turn of events finds himself interacting with the RAW chief Colonel Mahadev (Mammootty) known as Devil for his ruthless patriotism and comes to know about the God (Dino Maria) and his Mission Rabbit. To find out more about Mission Rabbit, Syndicate and how it is linked to Minister Jai Kishen (Sampath Raj) and what role Vidya (Sakshi Vaidya) played, enjoy Agent in action on the big screen.

    Agent Movie: Artists Review Akhil underwent a remarkable transformation for the role of Agent. He shed his loverboy image and showed variations in his expressions, dialogue delivery, and mannerisms. To the top of it, he enacted deadly, breathtaking, and risky stunts and action sequences. He also entertained delivering dialogues in a Telangana accent. He did everything that was required of him by the director and tried to carry the film on his shoulders. If he improves to showcase more intensity, he will reach the next level. He looked stylish and cool on the screen. Mammootty made his presence felt with his sedate performance. Though his screen presence is good, his role did not get much elevation. Dino Maria who played the antagonist role just passed through the motions. His role looked routine. Sakshi Vaidya looked hot and gorgeous on the screen. She got limited scope to showcase her acting talent. Sampath Raj and Varalakshmi Sarathkumar got limited roles. Others like Murali Sharma, Posani Krishna Murali, and Vikramjit played blink-and-miss roles. Urvashi Rautela did a special song and she looked raunchy as usual.
